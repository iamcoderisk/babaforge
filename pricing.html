Frequently Asked Questions

How is SendBaba more affordable?

We built our own custom SMTP infrastructure using modern Python technology. No expensive legacy systems, no unnecessary overhead. We pass the savings directly to you while maintaining premium quality.

What happens if I go over my limit?

We won't surprise you with overage fees! We'll send you a notification at 80% usage. If you exceed your limit, emails will queue until you upgrade, or you can enable auto-upgrade to the next tier.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the difference.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Absolutely! Non-profits, educational institutions, and open-source projects get 40% off any paid plan. Contact us with proof of status.

What about deliverability?

We maintain 99.9%+ delivery rates through TLS encryption, DKIM signing, SPF records, dedicated IPs, and direct relationships with major ISPs. Your emails reach the inbox, not spam.

Is there a setup fee?

Never. Zero setup fees. Zero cancellation fees. Zero migration fees. What you see is what you pay.

How does your free plan work?

2,000 emails/month, forever free. No credit card required. Full API access. Perfect for testing or small projects. Upgrade when you're ready.

Can I migrate from my current provider?

Yes! We offer free migration support with detailed documentation. Our team will help you move from Mailchimp, SendGrid, Mailgun, or any other provider. Zero downtime guaranteed.

Do you offer annual billing?
